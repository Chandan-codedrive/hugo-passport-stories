Kenya Highlights Safari | 8 Days

Kenya Highlights Safari | 8 Days

Nairobi •Maasai Mara • Lake Nakuru • Lake Naivasha • Amboseli

Fixed-Date Group Tour

This is a fixed-date group safari with a pre-defined itinerary covering Kenya’s most iconic wildlife and landscapes.

The tour is operated by our trusted local partner in Kenya. While we may not be physically present on this trip, the itinerary, routing, accommodations, and overall experience are carefully curated and coordinated by us to ensure reliability and quality.

🌟 Why Choose This Kenya Safari

  • Covers Kenya’s top national parks in one journey
  • Multiple game drives in Maasai Mara during peak wildlife season
  • Walking safari at Crescent Island for a unique experience
  • Rhino tracking at Lake Nakuru National Park
  • Amboseli safari with Mount Kilimanjaro views
  • Carefully planned drive times based on real road conditions
  • Mid-range lodges chosen for comfort and location

Day 1 | Arrival in Nairobi

Arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (NBO). Meet and greet with your driver and transfer to your hotel.

Afternoon at leisure or optional Nairobi city experiences such as Nairobi National Park or the Giraffe Centre.

Overnight in Nairobi.

Meals: As per hotel

Overnight: Tamarind Tree Hotel or similar (Mid-range)

Day 2 | Nairobi to Maasai Mara – Afternoon Game Drive

Early breakfast and depart for Maasai Mara via the Great Rift Valley. Scenic stop at the Rift Valley viewpoint for photography.

Arrive at the camp in time for lunch and check-in.

Afternoon to evening game drive with excellent chances of spotting lions, cheetahs, leopards, and plains game.

Overnight in Maasai Mara.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Overnight: Zebra Plains Mara Camp or similar

Day 3 | Maasai Mara – Full Day Game Drive

Full day safari in Maasai Mara with packed lunch.

Wildlife sightings may include wildebeest (seasonal migration), zebras, giraffes, antelope species, predators, hippos, and crocodiles along the Mara River.

Optional visit to a Maasai village in the afternoon or evening.

Overnight in Maasai Mara.

Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Dinner

Overnight: Zebra Plains Mara Camp or similar

Day 4 | Maasai Mara to Lake Nakuru

After breakfast, depart Maasai Mara and drive to Lake Nakuru National Park.

Afternoon game drive inside the park focusing on rhino sanctuary areas, giraffes, buffalo, flamingos, pelicans, and panoramic viewpoints like Baboon Cliff.

Exit the park and check in to your hotel in Nakuru town.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Overnight: Nakuru town hotel (Mid-range)

Day 5 | Lake Naivasha & Crescent Island to Nairobi

Early breakfast and drive to Lake Naivasha.

Boat ride to Crescent Island Game Sanctuary followed by a guided walking safari among giraffes, zebras, wildebeest, antelopes, and rich birdlife.

Lunch at Naivasha and then drive back to Nairobi.

Arrive in Nairobi by evening.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Overnight: Nairobi

Day 6 | Nairobi to Maasai Village & Amboseli

Late morning departure from Nairobi toward Amboseli.

En route visit to a Maasai village for cultural interaction including traditional dance and lifestyle insights.

Continue toward Amboseli area with stops at Lake Amboseli (seasonal salt lake) and a Kilimanjaro viewpoint, weather permitting.

Check in at your lodge outside Amboseli National Park and relax.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Overnight: Lodge near Amboseli National Park

Day 7 | Amboseli Morning Safari to Nairobi

Early morning game drive inside Amboseli National Park.

Excellent chances to see large elephant herds, lions, cheetahs, giraffes, hippos, and stunning Mount Kilimanjaro views.

Exit the park by midday and drive back to Nairobi, arriving by evening.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Day 8 | Departure

Transfer to the airport for your international flight back to India.

Meals: Breakfast (if applicable)
