Our Story

We are Mahulima and Kaustuv, the faces behind Passport Stories. We create travel content to inspire you and make planning your next adventure easier, helping you explore the world in a way that fits your style.

Over the years, we’ve explored the globe and collected memories from every corner. In Europe, we’ve visited all countries, capturing the Swiss Alps, Norwegian fjords, Santorini sunsets, hot air balloons over Cappadocia, Iceland’s dramatic landscapes, tulip fields in the Netherlands, Finland’s winter wonderlands, and romantic evenings in Paris.

In the Americas, we’ve journeyed through Chile, Argentina and Patagonia; Ecuador, Peru, and Bolivia; Brazil; all of Central America; 40 U.S. states; Mexico; and the Bahamas. In Africa, we’ve explored Morocco, Egypt, Namibia, Botswana, Zimbabwe, and Zambia, and even set foot on the icy continent of Antarctica.

In Asia, our travels have taken us to Israel, Jordan, Azerbaijan, Armenia, and beyond. Back home in India, we’ve discovered 23 states, each with its own culture, landscapes, and experiences.

Travel teaches lessons no book can. Through our stories, we aim to inspire you to step out of your comfort zone, explore new places, plan trips with confidence, and capture unforgettable memories. With years of global experience, we’ve learned the tips and tricks that make journeys smoother, more meaningful, and full of discovery — and now we want to share that with you.
